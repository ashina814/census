## Census

**Every mob in your world is someone.** Census gives villagers — and tagged
animals and monsters — a name, a personality, memories, a family, and feelings.
They remember what you do to them. Their friends hear about it. Their children
grow up knowing it. Nothing here is scripted; the stories **emerge**.

### What happens in a Census world

- Walk up to a villager and a small card shows who they are and how they feel.
  Right-click them with the **Census Book** for their full profile — personality,
  what they think of *you*, and their recent memories.
- Hurt one and they remember it. Hurt them enough and they flee on sight — the
  timid break first, the brave hold out. Word spreads: villagers who never saw
  it come to distrust you too.
- Be kind and it's also remembered. Win a villager over and one day they'll
  walk up and hand you a gift.
- Villagers form bonds, pair off for life, and raise children who inherit their
  parents' temperament. Kill someone's parent and a vengeful child — even one
  far away or asleep in an unloaded chunk — will come for you.
- Strong feelings show as particles; the fallen leave inscribed gravestones.

### Made to be extended

Tropes, behaviours, and which species are censused are all **datapack JSON** —
add your own stories and tune the simulation without writing code, live via
`/reload`.

### Notes

- Server-friendly: heavy work is spread across ticks and bounded; a single

  the config.
